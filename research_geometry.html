Geometry Modeling

Geometry modeling is the process of creating a 3D digital representation (i.e., a 3D model) of any object on computers. Although 3D models can be created manually, typically by artists and designers, through the use of commercial software such as 3DS Max, Maya, and Rhino, the complexity of the modeling task is generally beyond the ability of general users.

In this topic, our research focuses on creating 3D models automatically by developing novel algorithms. In particular, we are interested in image-based approaches that reconstruct a 3D model from color/depth images or user sketches, as well as data-driven approaches that generate a new 3D model by making use of existing ones.
