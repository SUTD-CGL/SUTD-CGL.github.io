3D Printable Robotics

3D printable robotics is an emerging research field that focuses on the inexpensive development of customizable robots by utilizing modern digital fabrication techniques like 3D printing. However, Robotic design is a time and skill intensive process, traditionally restricted to a very small group of experts. Research efforts in this field are devoted to making robotic design intuitive and accessible to everyone through computation, aiming to democratize robotics.

In this topic, we focus on developing algorithms and tools for design of a robotâ€™s mechanical, electrical, and software components for installation, prototyping, and validation. We are particularly interested in designing and fabricating small-scale robots that can perform user-specified motion and locomotion. These robots can be used in real-world applications like companion, micro-scale pick-and-place, search-and-rescue, and multi-agent systems.
