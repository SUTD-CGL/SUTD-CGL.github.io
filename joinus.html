Open Positions

We are currently looking for students and researchers in the general area of geometric computing, with a focus on modeling, processing, design and fabrication of digital 3D geometry. For more information about the positions and the lab, please contact Prof. Peng Song (peng_song@sutd.edu.sg).


PhD Students

Applicants should have a bachelor's degree or a master's degree in computer science, math, engineering, or related fields. Applications for the PhD program are centralized, please follow the procedure outlined at the SUTD website. Applicants are highly encouraged to apply for the Singapore International Graduate Award (SINGA), A*STAR CIS Scholarship (ACIS), or A*STAR Graduate Scholarship (AGS).


Visiting Students

Applicants should be students with major in computer science, math, engineering, or related fields and have an interest in doing high-quality research in computer graphics. Applications for visiting students can be sent by email to Prof. Peng Song (peng_song@sutd.edu.sg), including a CV and a list of publications.


Research Assistant (Computational Robotics)

Our lab has a Research Assistant position about computational design of 3D printable robots, which will be offered for 12 months. Applicants should hold a master's or bachelor’s degree in computer science, robotics, or a related field. Applicants are expected to have scientific knowledge of computer graphics, geometry modeling, or computational design.
