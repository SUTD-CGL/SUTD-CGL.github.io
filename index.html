Computer Graphics Laboratory



Welcome to the Computer Graphics Laboratory (CGL) at the Pillar of Information Systems Technology and Design (ISTD), Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), directed by Prof. Peng Song.

In CGL, we do research in computational design, digital fabrication, and geometry processing. We are particularly interested in developing computational methods, algorithms, and tools for designing fabricable and functional real-world objects. Take a look at our Research, Publications, and Youtube Channel to learn more.
