Welcome to the Computer Graphics Laboratory (CGL) at the Information Systems Technology and Design Pillar (ISTD), Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), directed by Prof. Peng Song.

CGL does research in the general area of geometric computing, with a focus on modeling, processing, design and fabrication of digital 3D geometry. Our goal is to understand fundamental principles of geometric computing, study efficient representations and data structures of complex geometry, and develop new algorithms and tools for their computational design, digital fabrication and advanced construction. The research developed in CGL has great potential to be used in architecture, robotics, mechanical engineering, education, and personal fabrication. Take a look at our Publications and Youtube Channel to learn more.
