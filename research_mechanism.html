Mechanism Design

Mechanism is a device that transforms input motions and forces into a desired set of output motions and forces. Mechanisms generally consist of moving components such as gears, linkages, and cams. Conventionally, the design of mechanisms is restricted to a very small group of professionals since the task requires extensive knowledge in engineering, 3D modeling, and simulation.

The goal of this topic is to develop computational methods and tools to enable casual users and assist professional users to design mechanisms according to the usersâ€™ high-level specifications, e.g., on the functionality. To achieve this goal, research efforts are devoted to abstraction of mechanisms, conceptual design, kinematic simulation, dynamic simulation, geometric optimization, and layout optimization.
